There are six types of operators in c++
1. Arithmatic operators --> + , - , / , * , ++ , -- , % 
2. Assignment operators --> = , += , -+ , /= , *= , %= 
3. Relational operators --> == , != , > , < , <= , >=
4. Logical operators --> ! , && , || 
5. Bitwise operators --> ~ , & , | , ^ , >> , << 
6. Special operators --> ?: , sizeof , & , -> , .
